Typing Practice

Similar to typeRacer's sudden death mode this typing practice game gets a random quote with at
with at least 250 characters and has the user type the quote. Upon the first mistake the user is
alerted they made a mistake and the page is reloaded. Upon completion the user is alerted of their
words per minute and then the page is reloaded.

Glizzy Royale

Glizzy Royale is a browser based multiplayer game inspired by internet memes and the popular phone game,
Brawl Stars. Players connect through the browser and get put into an arena with all other players currently connected
to the server. In the arena players battle in a free for all style as hot dogs (glizzies) and can fire ketchup blobs to
deal damage to other players. When a player's health is running low the golden glizzy in the center of the arena dispenses
bread crumbs to replenish players' buns (health). Written in C# using Unity Game Engine and was hosted on an apache server.
