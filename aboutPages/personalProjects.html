Similar to typeRacer's sudden death mode this typing practice game gets a random quote with at

alerted they made a mistake and the page is reloaded. Upon completion the user is alerted of their

Reduced Row Echelon Form Calculator

into reduced row echelon form. My profoessor only knows how to do this on a few graphing calculators
and the TI-Nspire is not one of them. That is the calculator I have and so I wrote a Gauss-Jordan
algorithm to use on homework instead of having to do my work by hand.
