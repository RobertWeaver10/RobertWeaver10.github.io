About the Word Guessing Game

This game is ran in the browser console. The game works by picking a random 6 letter word from a dictionary and then searches the dictionary for all words 3 to 6 letters long that can be made from the letters of the initially picked word. The player's goal is to guess all the anagrams, and if they get stuck they can either scramble the letters of the root word or give up in which case all the words get revealed.
